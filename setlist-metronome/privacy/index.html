Setlist Metronome does not require sign-up, does not use user accounts, and does not send information to our servers. We do not collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, or any personal identifier;

All content generated in the app (e.g. lists and settings) is stored locally on your device, according to your operating system. We have no access to that information.

The app does not integrate analytics, ads, or social features that collect data on our behalf. If this changes, this page will be updated before a new version is published.

4. Children

Because we do not collect data, use of the app by minors does not involve processing of personal information by the developer.

5. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app. The date at the top shows the latest version.
